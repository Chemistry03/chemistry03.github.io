Welcome!

Ms. Kavita Narang is a dedicated researcher and educator in the field of Supramolecular Chemistry. She is currently a research scholar at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati and an Assistant Professor at Government SukhRam Nage College Nagri, Chhattishgarh

Ms. Narang's research interests include the development of new supramolecular materials for various applications, including drug delivery and organic electronics. She has published numerous papers in high-impact journals and has presented her work at several international conferences.

Research Interests

Dr. Narang's research focuses on the design, synthesis, and characterization of supramolecular materials with potential applications in various fields. Her current work includes the development of new supramolecular materials for drug delivery, organic electronics, and catalysis. She uses a variety of techniques to study these materials, including X-ray crystallography, NMR spectroscopy, and computational methods.

Ms. Narang is also passionate about teaching and mentoring students. She is committed to fostering an inclusive and collaborative learning environment that helps students develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
